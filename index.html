Research (2014–2018)

In my previous life, I was a PhD researcher in geometric group theory. I specialised in studying Thompson's groups, culminating in my thesis Conjugacy and centralisers in Thompson's group T. As part of this, I wrote the thompson Python package.

My thesis was never published as research articles, but I did contribute to The power conjugacy problem in Higman-Thompson groups (arχiv).
